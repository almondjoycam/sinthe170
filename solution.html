Presenting a Solution

What approaches can we take?

Harm reduction is an approach to drug use that aims to give substance users autonomy to live healthily. This can be considered the opposite of punitive drug policy, which criminalizes drug possession and use, and pushes substance users into a repeating cycle of incarceration and withdrawal.

Some harm reduction-focused solutions the Drug Policy Alliance, a US organization, is exploring include legal regulation and safer supply. Proponents of this approach explain further that unregulated and illegal drug suppliers often sell mixed or impure products, and sometimes even falsely advertise products as a substance they are not. Legal regulation of a drug distribution market could significantly reduce false advertising, and safer supply of drug manufacturing could prevent impurities or unknown additives in products. Effects caused by combining two drugs in the body are called interactions, and sometimes, interactions can be life-threatening or fatal. By reducing the potential of these unknown interactions in drug suppliers, the risk of fatal interactions is also greatly reduced.

In Fontaine, the main character learns through the region's quest that a man named Callas had been attempting to ban Sinthe entirely from the village of Poisson three years before the game's events. Poisson is a town that is located entirely underground, seemingly within a sewer system of metal piping, and is almost entirely comprised of Fontaine's working-class population. Callas had been in a government position within Poisson at that time, and had been in contact with one of the Sinthe dealers to try to gain information to help facilitate the shutting down of Sinthe manufacture. He was able to gain the location of the warehouse where Sinthe was being produced, but couldn't act on the information before he left office and died. By following the quest, the main character ties up this loose thread by visiting the warehouse location and collecting incriminating evidence in order to take legal action. Fortunately, there was only one man behind the operation, and by apprehending him, the entire production was halted. However, because no more Sinthe is being produced, the principles of legal regulation and safer supply can't exactly be applied.
